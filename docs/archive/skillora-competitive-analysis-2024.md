# Skillora Competitive Analysis

## Executive Summary
Skillora is a direct competitor to Interview Optimiser, offering AI-powered interview practice with a focus on both B2C (job seekers) and B2B (recruiters/educational institutions) markets. This analysis provides actionable insights to enhance Interview Optimiser's competitive positioning.

## Key Findings

### 1. Landing Page & User Journey

#### Strengths to Emulate:
- **Trust Indicators**: Prominent display of logos from 56+ companies where their users work (Google, Microsoft, Amazon, etc.)
- **Social Proof**: Video testimonials, LinkedIn testimonials, and detailed user reviews with names, positions, and locations
- **Clear Value Proposition**: "AI Interviewer to help you get your dream job" - simple and direct
- **Multiple CTAs**: "Try for free" appears multiple times with consistent messaging
- **Microsoft for Startups Badge**: Adds credibility and enterprise validation

#### Improvements for Interview Optimiser:
1. Add a logo carousel showing companies where users work
2. Implement video testimonials and LinkedIn integration
3. Create a more prominent "Trusted by X users" section
4. Add Microsoft/AWS/Google startup program badges if applicable

### 2. Product Features

#### Skillora's Key Features:
- **4 Interview Types**: Job-based, Skill-based, Resume-based, Behavioral
- **AI Interview Customization**: Target company, industry, experience level
- **Resume Optimization**: ATS score checker, keyword optimization, job match analysis
- **Question Bank**: Comprehensive question database with example answers
- **Video Recording**: Optional video recording for premium tiers
- **Instant Feedback**: Real-time analysis with example answers

#### Competitive Advantages Interview Optimiser Should Leverage:
- **Voice-to-Voice Focus**: Skillora doesn't emphasize real-time voice interaction
- **Prosody Analysis**: Our unique differentiator not offered by Skillora
- **Hume AI Integration**: Advanced emotional intelligence not visible in Skillora

### 3. Pricing Strategy

#### Skillora's Pricing Tiers:
1. **Essential**: $8/month (50% off from $16) - 4 interviews with video
2. **Premium**: $15/month (50% off from $30) - Unlimited without video
3. **Infinity**: $30/month (60% off from $60) - Unlimited with video
4. **Enterprise**: Custom pricing

#### Pricing Insights:
- Always showing "discounted" prices creates urgency
- Clear feature differentiation between tiers
- Video recording as premium feature differentiator
- Free trial prominently advertised

### 4. B2B Market Approach

#### Skillora's B2B Features:
- **For Recruiters**: 
  - Secure & proctored AI interviews
  - 10x faster hiring cycles claim
  - In-depth skill analysis
  - ATS & HRIS integration
  
- **For Educational Institutions**:
  - Customizable scenarios
  - Progress tracking
  - Placement rate improvement metrics
  
- **White Label Solutions**: Complete branding customization

#### B2B Strategy Recommendations:
1. Develop clear value propositions for each B2B segment
2. Create dedicated landing pages for recruiters and educational institutions
3. Implement white-label capabilities
4. Build ATS integrations (Greenhouse, Lever, Workday)

### 5. UI/UX Patterns

#### Design Elements to Adopt:
- **Clean Navigation**: Simple top nav with clear sections
- **Interactive Elements**: Hover states, smooth animations
- **Progress Indicators**: Visual cues for multi-step processes
- **Chat Widget**: Customer support accessibility
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach evident
