3. Interview Optimiser: How We Solve It

3.1 Live, Adaptive AI Interviewing
• Speech-to-speech conversation: Candidates think on their feet—no pause button, no rehearse-and-retake loophole.
• Dynamic follow-ups: Question difficulty adjusts in real time, revealing genuine ceilings (or lack thereof).
• Built-in compliance: Standardised scoring rubrics and audit trails reduce bias, support DEI goals, and simplify regulator reporting.

3.2 “Shift-Left” Screening at Unlimited Scale
• Launch a job → share link → every applicant receives the same high-fidelity interview within hours, not weeks.
• Zero scheduling friction; your team reviews recordings and rich analytics instead of hosting calls.

3.3 360° Candidate Analytics
• Full recording & transcript with keyword tagging.
• Prosody and confidence metrics (speech rate, fillers, sentiment).
• Question-level scoring mapped to your competency model.
• Export into ATS or HRIS in one click.

3.4 Consumer Practice Tier = Built-In Talent Pipeline
• 15 min/day free practice keeps tens of thousands of candidates engaged.
• Those users train and tune our models, ensuring employer interviews feel natural and current.
• opt-in résumé pool offers brand exposure and pre-warmed talent for your openings.
